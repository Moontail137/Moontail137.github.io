Introduction to Animation

Animation has been around for centuries in many forms, however animation as we know it today saw most of its greatest developments in the past 100 years.

From hand drawn, to stop motion, to CGI, animation and it’s history are incredibly interesting, and to learn more about current and future advancements, we must first take a step back to appreciate it’s history.
